Welcome to the Chicago Parks Reservations Dashboard

  • Discover the vibrant public life of Chicago through our interactive dashboard, designed to provide a detailed visual account of the diverse events held across the city's parks. This tool offers anyone from city planners to local residents a window into the rich tapestry of community activities, from concerts and festivals to educational programs and sports events.
  • Explore event trends over the years, identify seasonal preferences, and gain insights into the most popular parks for gatherings. Whether you're planning an event or simply curious about city activities, our dashboard serves as your guide to Chicago's public events landscape.
  • Utilizing cutting-edge data visualization techniques, our dashboard presents complex data in an intuitive format, making it easy to navigate and understand. Interactive elements allow users to filter information based on specific criteria, such as time periods, event types, or park locations, providing a customized view that caters to individual needs or interests.
  • Join us in exploring Chicago's community events through this dynamic and insightful tool, and see how public spaces come alive throughout the year!

How to Use the Dashboard:

  1. Step 1: Click the 'View Dashboard' button to access the dashboard on GitHub.
  2. Step 2: Download the Jupyter Notebook associated with the dashboard.
  3. Step 3: Upload the Jupyter Notebook to your JupyterLab or any Jupyter Notebook environment.
  4. Step 4: Run the code cells within the Jupyter Notebook to activate the dashboard.
  5. Step 5: Enjoy the visualization, interact with it, and discover interesting features and trends about park events in Chicago.
